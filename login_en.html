How to login in with QR Code

Please scan QR Code on the web page or click the below logos with your mobile phone's camera to download the app. After the installation of the app, please follow the instruction of the app to login in TAITRA member's account and complete the process of linking the account to your mobile phone. Then, please click the account's code scanner to scan the login QR Code. You can login in to TAITRA's website's services using the authenticated device without entering your TAITRA's account and password.
